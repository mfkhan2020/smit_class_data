Prawn Pilau Biryani Rice

Ingredients
  • 1 ½ tbsp (22 ½ ml) oil
  • ½ (75 g) large onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • ½ inch ginger minced
  • ½ medium stick whole cinnamon
  • 1 ½ (185 g) medium tomatoes very finely diced
  • 1 cups (200 g) basmati rice (washed and soaked for at least 30 minutes before using)
  • 6 oz (175 g) raw king prawns
  • 3 cups/750ml water (IMPORTANT: please read notes if you are changing the serving size)
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp ground coriander
  • ½ tsp black pepper coarsely ground is best
Instructions
  1. Heat oil in a pot and add the onions, cinnamon, garlic and ginger. Sauté till golden brown.
  2. Add the tomatoes and spices and a splash of water. Cook till the tomatoes break down into a soft mix.
  3. Add the rice, prawns and water. Bring to a boil.
  4. Once at a boil, allow the rice and all the ingredients to cook together.
  5. After 10 minutes, 90% of the water will have evaporated. Cover and bring the heat to the lowest setting. Allow this to steam for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Fluff up before serving.
